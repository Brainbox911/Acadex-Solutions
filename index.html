About the Brand

What Acadex Solutions Stands For

Acadex Solutions is a student-focused academic and technology brand created to provide reliable support services for students across Nigerian higher institutions. The brand helps students handle academic research, project development, technology tasks, creative designs, and documentation with a professional standard.

Our aim is to reduce the stress students face during project writing, seminar preparation, coding work, research formatting, and digital assignments by providing structured, affordable, and quality-driven solutions.

Our Mission

To provide quality academic and digital solutions that help students succeed through professionalism, innovation, and excellence.

Meet the CEO

Suleiman Nasiru Ozomarisi

Founder & CEO, Acadex Solutions

Suleiman Nasiru Ozomarisi is a student of Federal University Lokoja, from the Department of Mathematics. He is the founder of Acadex Solutions, a student-focused academic and technology brand created to support students across Nigerian universities and higher institutions.

The idea behind Acadex Solutions came from the need to make academic and digital support easier, faster, and more accessible for students. Many students struggle with research writing, project formatting, computing projects, presentations, designs, and other academic tasks. Acadex Solutions was created to reduce that stress by providing reliable academic, creative, and technology-based services in one place.

As a professional graphic designer, UI/UX designer, web developer, motion graphics designer, video editor, and strong mathematics student, Suleiman brings together creativity, technology, and academic understanding. His goal is to help students present their ideas professionally, improve their academic work, and gain access to practical digital solutions that can support their education and future career.
